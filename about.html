About me

Welcome to Kikiarts, where passion meets vibrant creativity! I'm from Lawrence Mass, a devoted artist who has been enamored with the world of art since childhood. Holding a bachelor's degree in architecture and illustration from Mass Art, I embarked on my artistic journey and graduated in 2021.

In the midst of the global pandemic, I made a pivotal decision to elevate my passion for art into a thriving career. Thus, Kikiarts was born, blossoming in the humble confines of my parent's apartment basement and steadily evolving over the years. My heartfelt gratitude extends to the incredible supporters and the local community that has served as a wellspring of inspiration for my artistic endeavors.

I am a firm believer in the power of color, and my artwork reflects this with a vibrant explosion of hues. Refusing to be confined to the monochrome, I challenge the norm with a diverse and lively palette. This commitment to breaking artistic boundaries has garnered support not only from my local community but also from fellow businesses.

As a proud Caribbean woman and owner of an art business, I am eager to forge ahead into an exciting future. The unwavering support from my community and the collaborations with local businesses have fueled my ambition. With each stroke of color, I am weaving a narrative that transcends boundaries and celebrates the richness of diversity.

Thank you for joining me on this artistic journey. Together, let's paint a future filled with endless possibilities and a kaleidoscope of creativity.
